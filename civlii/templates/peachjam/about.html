{% blocktrans trimmed %} JuriCIV is an Ivorian legal information platform developed as part of the Open Law Africa programme, in collaboration with Laws.Africa and the Côte d'Ivoire Bar Association.
Developed as part of GIZ PRoLA Phase 2, the platform initially focuses on business law, arbitration, OHADA resources, and selected commercial court decisions. Over time, JuriCIV aims to become a reliable point of access to legal information in Côte d'Ivoire, in support of legal certainty, access to justice, and the rule of law. {% endblocktrans %}

Laws.Africa

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Laws.Africaopens in new tab is a South Africa non-profit organisation that digitises African legal information for public use. Open access to digital African legal information helps communities to thrive, businesses to succeed, and judicial officers and civil servants to deliver services efficiently and effectively. {% endblocktrans %}
